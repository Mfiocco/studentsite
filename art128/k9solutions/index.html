ABOUT

K9 Solutions is a dog training and canine education company based out of Honolulu, Oahu. The company has been around since 2023 and was created by a skillful canine educator, Max Lee. His training focuses on building and strengthening each client’s relationship to their furry companion.

Max sets his clients up for success by viewing dog training through a realistic lens; using realistic techniques while setting realistic boundaries and realistic expectations for you and your dog.

Our core philosophy:
Relationship-Based Training
Balanced Techniques
Fair and Ethical
