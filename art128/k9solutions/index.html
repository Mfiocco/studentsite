ABOUT

K9 Solutions is a dog training and canine education company based out of Honolulu, Oahu. The company has been around since 2023 and was created by a skillful canine educator, Max Lee. His training focuses on building and strengthening each client’s relationship to their furry companion.

Max sets his clients up for success by viewing dog training through a realistic lens; using realistic techniques while setting realistic boundaries and realistic expectations for you and your dog.

Our core philosophy:
Relationship-Based Training
Balanced Techniques
Fair and Ethical

SERVICES

Private Sessions

Private dog training sessions offer personalized attention and tailored strategies, ensuring efficient learning and addressing specific behavioral concerns effectively.

Basic Leash Training

Basic dog leash training instills crucial obedience skills and strengthens the bond between owner and dog, fostering safer and more enjoyable walks while promoting overall control and security.

Advanced Obedience

Advanced obedience dog training enhances your canine companion's responsiveness to commands, elevating their discipline and reliability in diverse environments, ultimately facilitating seamless integration into various real-life situations.

House Sitting Dog

House sitting for dogs provides them with familiar surroundings, reduces stress associated with being away from home, and gives owners peace of mind knowing their beloved pets are receiving personalized care and attention in a familiar environment.

Walking/Drop-in (Kaimuki Clients*)

Dog walking promotes physical exercise, mental stimulation, and socialization for dogs, contributing to their overall health, happiness, and well-being in a structured environment.

Resource Guarding

Training a dog through resource guarding is crucial to prevent potential conflicts, ensure safety, and promote a trusting relationship between the dog and their human family.

Separation Anxiety

Training a dog with separation anxiety can alleviate distress, promote independence, and foster a sense of security, leading to improved behavior and a happier, more balanced relationship between the dog and their owner.

Reactivity and Aggression

Training a dog with reactivity and aggression can help manage their behavior, reduce stress and fear, and improve their ability to interact calmly and positively with people and other animals.

Behavior Modification

Behavioral modification training for dogs offers tailored interventions to address specific issues, fostering positive changes in behavior, promoting better communication between the dog and their owner, and ultimately enhancing the dog's overall well-being.

Socialization and Exposure

Socialization and exposure training for dogs facilitate adaptability, confidence, and positive interactions with various environments, people, and animals, ensuring a well-rounded, emotionally balanced companion capable of navigating diverse situations with ease.
